Status Effect Icons
  • Hemorrhage
    Techniques designed to wound and exsanguinate can inflict acute Blood Loss.
  • Frostbite
    Cold and Freezing techniques can inflict Frostbite.
  • Poison
    Toxins designed to whittle and slowly sap the life from enemies can inflict Poison or Deadly Poison.
  • Scarlet Rot
    Pathogens designed to crumble the very being of its enemies and consume their flesh can inflict Scarlet Rot.
